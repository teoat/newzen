The engine automatically matches internal ledgers against bank statements using a weighted multi-variable algorithm.

- **Tier 1 (Exact)**: 100% Match on Amount + (Date OR Ref). Auto-confirmed.
- **Tier 2 (Strong)**: >95% Confidence. Minor date drift (Â±3 days). Auto-confirmed.
- **Tier 3 (Partial)**: 85-95%. Requires manual review.
- **Tier 4 (Weak)**: <85%. Flagged for investigation.
